Bring Back the Pack!

Petition by Eric Martinez

Disallowing students from using backpacks is unaccommodating and unnecessary for many reasons:

  • Many students arrive to school already late to class due to buses, making going to their locker an unintuitive, class-disrupting decision.
  • Online textbooks are not always available, so students need to carry both textbooks, tablets, and notebooks with them from class to class to avoid being tardy.
  • Backpacks are NOT a tripping hazard, such as tablet charging cables and stacks of books strewn about the classroom floor, when desks have a hook to hang the bags from.
  • Students who take their materials to and from school in a backpack will not have room to fit them in their lockers during the day, considering the fact that the lockers are about one foot wide and only slightly deeper.
  • Needing to share lockers due to a lack of space reveals poor planning and an unempathetic stance on the situation.
